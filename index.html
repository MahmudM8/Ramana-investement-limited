About Us

Learn about our history, mission, and vision

Our History

Founded over 20 years ago, Ramana Investment Limited has established itself as a trusted partner in contracting and supply services. Our journey began with a simple mission: to deliver exceptional service with integrity and professionalism.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality contracting and supply services that meet the evolving needs of our clients while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and ethical conduct.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of contracting and supply services in Nigeria, recognized for our reliability, quality, and commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.

Building Contracts

We undertake construction projects of all scales, delivering quality workmanship on time and within budget. Our expertise spans residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Supply Contracts
